Crafting intuitive digital experiences that connect people and technology.

I'm a UX Designer who's spent the last 16 years teaching in primary schools, obsessing with how people learn, navigate, and solve problems. I love diving into the 'why' behind user behaviour and turning messy, complex information into something that feels simple and intuitive.

For me, design is all about empathy and common sense; I'm a firm believer in data-driven iteration and making sure every pixel serves a real human purpose. I'm always looking for ways to bridge the gap between technical systems and the people who use them to create products that genuinely make life a bit easier.
